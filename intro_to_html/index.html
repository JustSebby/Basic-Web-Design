Five Nights At Freddy's

In Five Nights at Freddy's you are playing as a night security guard by the name of Mike Schmidt working at a family pizza restaurant named Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. At the start of your night shifts you get a daily call to instruct you on the nights. You have access to cameras to know the animatronics moves and doors not let the animatronics into your room and jumpscare you which will result in a game over.

Chararcters

The Chararcters include of Phone Guy,Mike Schmidt the nightguard which is you the player, Freddy Fazbear is the leader of the band and he's a brown bear with a black hat and bowtie,Bonnie the Bunny is a purple bunny who's the guitarist of the band. Chica The Chicken is the lady of the group and a backup singer, Foxy the Pirate Fox is a eyed fox with a hook for his right hand and last but not least Golden Freddy which is a yellow version of Freddy.

Random Facts
  • Scott Cawthon (The Creator of FNaF) conceived the idea of FNaF based on criticism of his previous game, Chipper & Sons Lumber Co. Reviewers complained on frightening chararcters that had animatronic-like movement which inspired Scott Cawthon to create a scarier experience.
  • The Concept of FNaF is simiar to a 2006 comedy film "Night at the Museum.
  • Scott Cawthon was scared of Bonnie as he had plenty of nightmares when developing FNaF 1.
  • On rare occasions when starting up the game or after the game over screen there's a 0.1% of an eyeless Bonnie to pop up for 10 seconds until white pupils appear.
